This was a very interesting and eventful year.

The year started like no other. With a new disease on my shoulder, a lot of changes in my friend circle, and some 42 stress, it sounds like something nobody would want to go through again.

But this year was still amazing. It was just one thing after another. So much happened, and I discovered so many new things.

From moving out of my parents' house, playing tunes for parties, still being good at video games :P, to visiting all kinds of places through hackathons and getting insight into how hard CTF challenges can be.

All in all, I give this year a solid Green on a scale from crow to 11.
